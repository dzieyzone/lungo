CS COMPACT™ & CS COMPACT™ EA Circular Staplers

The Chex™ CS COMPACT™ range was developed with one of the world's leading Specialist centres in Colon and Rectal Surgery. The resultant range of circular staplers creates devices that make anastomosis of the rectum easier. The CS COMPACT™ is a SHORT-STRAIGHT Circular Stapler 34.5mm long, that in many Low and especially Ultra-Low Anterior resections will feel right for you.
