April 2013 - Introducing Federico Bilotti - Director of Innovation and Manufacturing (Europe)

Federico Bilotti has more than three decades worth of experience in the Medical Device Industry, formerly being the Director of Innovation for Ethicon GmbH in Hamburg, Germany and then for Johnson & Johnson Med Spa in Rome, Italy, also being the former R&D Team Leader for Ethicon Endo Surgery Inc. in Cincinnati, Ohio USA - he led the innovation that created many of Ethicon Endo-Surgery's most successful and lucrative products, procedures and manufacturing initiatives. These include Contour (treatment of rectal pathologies), Transtarr (for endoluminal treatment of ODS), PPH (for treatment of hemorrhoidal pathologies), Linear Staplers and Flexible Staplers (for resection of pathologies from thoracic and abdominalorgans),Reusable Needle Holder(for laparoscopic suturing), Clip Appliers (for the ligation of vessels), Innovative Staple Forms (to increase hemostasis) and more.

We welcome Federico to Frankenman International and look forward to sharing his wealth of experience in the creation of new procedure enabling technologies for Frankenman International.
